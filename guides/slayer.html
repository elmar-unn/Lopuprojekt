Slayer Guide

All 6 slayer types — unlock order, boss tiers, key drops, and the RNG Meter system.

v0.21

Slayers are instanced boss fights you start by spending coins at a Slayer NPC. Each kill gives Slayer XP, and reaching certain levels unlocks new content, recipes, and stat bonuses. Each boss has T1–T5 tiers with increasing HP and rewards.

Unlock Order

Slayers unlock in a chain — you need to progress through earlier ones to reach the later ones. The recommended order for progression is below.

Zombie — Revenant Horror

Start here. T1 is very cheap and takes seconds. The Wand of Healing drop at level 3 is a major QoL item — it lets you heal yourself during slayer quests. Push to level 7 before worrying about other slayers.

  • Key drops: Revenant Flesh, Undead Catalyst, Wand of Healing (rare), Revenant Catalyst
  • Stat bonuses: +2 to +6 Health per level
  • Worth noting: T5 boss (Atoned Horror) has mechanics that require specific gear to damage — check guides before attempting

Spider — Tarantula Broodfather

Tarantula Armor crafted from Spider slayer drops is a strong early-mid game armor set for Berserkers. T4 is efficient for farming drops.

  • Key drops: Tarantula Web, Tarantula Silk, Tarantula Helmet (level 5 recipe)
  • Stat bonuses: +1 to +3 Crit Damage per level
  • Worth noting: Digested Mosquito drops from minibosses — needed for Spider Artifact

Wolf — Sven Packmaster

Wolf unlocks Enderman slayer at T4, so push to T4 quickly. The drops aren't the most valuable but you need the level.

  • Key drops: Wolf Tooth, Golden Tooth, Wolf Coat, Overflux Power Orb (rare)
  • Stat bonuses: Speed and Health increases per level
  • Worth noting: Overflux Power Orb is a strong accessory for any build

Enderman — Voidgloom Seraph

Enderman slayer is one of the most important for late-game progression. Etherwarp Conduit (T3 drop) is a massive mobility upgrade that lets you teleport to blocks you're looking at. Ender Artifact is also here.

  • Key drops: Null Sphere, Etherwarp Conduit (T3+), Ender Artifact, Enderman Cortex Rewriter
  • Stat bonuses: Health and Intelligence per level
  • Worth noting: Enderman Slayer level 9 unlocks a unique perk. T3 fills the RNG Meter — see below.

Blaze — Inferno Demonlord

Blaze drops Mana Disintegrator and Gauntlet of Contagion, both critical for Mage builds. The boss has a complex mechanic involving shooting specific orbs at the right time.

  • Key drops: Derelict Ashe, Mana Disintegrator, Gauntlet of Contagion, Kelvin Inverter
  • Stat bonuses: Intelligence and Fire resistance per level
  • Worth noting: The boss requires hitting orbs in the correct order — wrong order heals the boss

Vampire — Riftstalker Bloodfiend

Accessed through The Rift dimension. Vampire slayer requires Rift-specific mechanics and drops Bloodfiend Armor, which is strong in the Rift.

  • Key drops: Coven Seal, Vampire Fang, Bloodfiend Armor pieces, Sanguine armor
  • Worth noting: The Rift has its own separate stats and mechanics — Rift gear is not usable outside

RNG Meter

Every T3+ boss kill fills an RNG Meter. When the meter is full, the next kill is guaranteed to drop the rare item for that slayer. This makes T3 significantly better for farming specific drops than T4 or T5 in many cases.

Always fill the RNG Meter before killing the boss. Start the quest, kill enough regular mobs to fill the meter, then summon. This guarantees the rare drop on that run.

Combat Wisdom

Killing a new boss type at T1, T2, and T3 each gives +1 Combat Wisdom. T4 and T5 give +2 each. Maximum total from slayers is +34 Combat Wisdom across all six types at all tiers. Combat Wisdom increases the rate at which you gain Combat XP.
