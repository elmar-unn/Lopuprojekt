Progression Guidebook

SkyBlock progression from level 0 to endgame. Skills, gear, weapons, and ways to make coins at each stage.

Updated May 2026 v0.21

First Skills

To start off you should pick a skill thats intresting to you and focus only that skill until you hit a certain point. Your first choice should be either foraging, combat or mining. For foraging you should get into the galatea and get at least Heart of the Forest Tier III. For combat you should get combat level XII and possibly the ender armor or glacite armor. For mining you want to reach the dwarven mines and unlock your Heart of the Mountain.

Early Mobility and Damage

Your first mobility tools is probably the grappling hook (around 5k on the AH). Your focus should be a Aspect of the End which is gonna be your main mobility tool for the entire game. For damage the easiest way is to reforge your weapon and armor. You should always have 100% crit chance and the best reforge for that is sharp on your weapon and fierce on your armor.

Magical Power

To unlock your magical power you need to get some redstone collection. Easiest way to do that is to get some redstone minions and smack some super compactors in them otherwise you can manually mine which is more tedious. This part is important for accessory slots. The cheapest way to get your magical power up is to use the skyhelper discord bot that tells you which accessory is best price to MP.

Early Coins

The best way to make money early game is to take part of an event like Year of the Seal and Grand Feast. These are the easiest way to get money you can make 40m/h from just bouncing the balloons for the seal treats. Otherwise a permanent way to make money is bazaar flipping - here is a link to a guide video
